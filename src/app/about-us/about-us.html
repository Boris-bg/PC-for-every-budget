Our Story

E very great idea starts with a simple question. “Why should powerful computers be a luxury?”

In 2025, Boris Georgiev — a passionate hardware enthusiast — set out to make performance accessible to everyone, from students building their first PC to professionals looking for reliable power without the premium price tag.

“What started as a small garage project helping friends assemble affordable builds soon evolved into a mission — bringing powerful, reliable computers within everyone’s reach.”

Today, PC for Every Budget is a trusted online destination for tech lovers across the region. We’re proud to offer:

  • 💻 Custom-built PCs for gamers, creators, and everyday users
  • 🧩 Quality components from top-tier brands
  • 🛠️ Expert support tailored to every budget
  • 🚚 Fast delivery and reliable customer service
